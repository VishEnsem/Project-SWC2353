Mee Goreng Mamak (Mamak-style Fried Noodles)
Preparation: 10 mins, Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • Yellow noodles - 200g
  • Tofu - 50g, cubed and fried
  • Shrimp - 100g, peeled
  • Eggs - 1
  • Soy sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Tomato sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Green onion - for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add shrimp.
  2. Crack an egg and scramble it.
  3. Add noodles, tofu, soy sauce, and tomato sauce.
  4. Stir-fry for 5 minutes, then garnish with green onion.
