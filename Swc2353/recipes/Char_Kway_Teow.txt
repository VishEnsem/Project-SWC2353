Title: Char Kway Teow
Image: https://images.deliveryhero.io/image/fd-my/LH/b7pl-listing.jpg
Time: Preparation: 10 mins, Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients:
- Flat rice noodles - 200g
- Shrimp - 100g, peeled
- Bean sprouts - a handful
- Chinese chives - chopped
- Eggs - 1
- Soy sauce - 2 tbsp
- Chili paste - 1 tsp
- Garlic - 2 cloves, minced

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a wok and fry garlic until fragrant.
2. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
3. Push shrimp aside, crack in the egg, and scramble.
4. Add noodles, soy sauce, chili paste, bean sprouts, and chives.
5. Stir-fry for a few minutes and serve hot.
