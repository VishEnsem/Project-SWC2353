Title: Spaghetti Carbonara
Image: https://www.vincenzosplate.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/1500x1500-Photo-26_2489-How-to-Make-Spaghetti-alla-Carbonara-in-2023-V1.jpg
Time: Preparation: 10 mins, Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients:
- Spaghetti - 200g
- Eggs - 2
- Pancetta - 100g
- Parmesan Cheese - 50g
- Black Pepper - to taste

Instructions:
1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
2. Cook the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and add cheese.
4. Mix spaghetti with pancetta, then add egg mixture.
5. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
