Title: Roti Jala (Lace Pancakes)
Image: https://ordinarybaker.com.my/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/IMG_1506-1-scaled.jpeg
Time: Preparation: 10 mins, Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients:
- Flour - 1 cup
- Coconut milk - 1 cup
- Eggs - 1
- Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
- Salt - 1/4 tsp

Instructions:
1. Mix flour, coconut milk, egg, turmeric powder, and salt until smooth.
2. Pour batter into a squirt bottle or roti jala mold.
3. Heat a non-stick pan and create lace patterns with the batter.
4. Cook for 1-2 minutes, then fold and serve with curry.
