Title: Ikan Bakar (Grilled Fish)
Image: https://magazine.foodpanda.my/wp-content/uploads/sites/12/2019/10/cropped-Ikan-Bakar.jpg
Time: Preparation: 15 mins, Cooking: 20 mins

Ingredients:
- Fish - 1 whole, cleaned
- Turmeric powder - 1 tsp
- Salt - to taste
- Lime - 1, juiced
- Banana leaf - for wrapping

Instructions:
1. Rub fish with turmeric powder, salt, and lime juice.
2. Wrap fish in a banana leaf and grill for 20 minutes.
3. Serve with sambal or chili sauce.
