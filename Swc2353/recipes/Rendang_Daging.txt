Title: Rendang Daging (Beef Rendang)
Image: https://asset.kompas.com/crops/qnQtJh5Q3qtrKL91mTLGAjf0Tf4=/0x0:1000x667/1200x800/data/photo/2023/03/25/641e5ef63dea4.jpg
Time: Preparation: 20 mins, Cooking: 2 hours

Ingredients:
- Beef - 500g, cubed
- Coconut milk - 400ml
- Lemongrass - 2 stalks, bruised
- Turmeric leaf - 1, shredded
- Curry leaves - 5-6
- Galangal - 1 inch, smashed
- Salt - to taste

Instructions:
1. Blend shallots, garlic, ginger, and chilies into a paste.
2. Heat oil and cook the paste until fragrant.
3. Add beef, coconut milk, lemongrass, turmeric leaf, curry leaves, and galangal.
4. Simmer on low heat for 2 hours, stirring occasionally until the beef is tender.
5. Season with salt and serve.
