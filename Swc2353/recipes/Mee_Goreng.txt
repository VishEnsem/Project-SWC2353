Title: Mee Goreng Mamak (Mamak-style Fried Noodles)
Image: https://d3ih4j2n5zotw5.cloudfront.net/uploads/all/UuszcUk9C5q7KeKWOjUgwLM4tZXy47DZ75LKJDvc.jpg
Time: Preparation: 10 mins, Cooking: 10 mins

Ingredients:
- Yellow noodles - 200g
- Tofu - 50g, cubed and fried
- Shrimp - 100g, peeled
- Eggs - 1
- Soy sauce - 1 tbsp
- Tomato sauce - 1 tbsp
- Green onion - for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan and add shrimp.
2. Crack an egg and scramble it.
3. Add noodles, tofu, soy sauce, and tomato sauce.
4. Stir-fry for 5 minutes, then garnish with green onion.
