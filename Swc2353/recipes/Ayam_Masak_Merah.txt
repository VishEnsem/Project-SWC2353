Title: Ayam Masak Merah (Red-cooked Chicken)
Image: https://cdn.kuali.com/wp-content/uploads/2004/01/08111532/175225445_m.jpg
Time: Preparation: 15 mins, Cooking: 25 mins

Ingredients:
- Chicken drumsticks - 4
- Tomato sauce - 1/2 cup
- Chili paste - 2 tbsp
- Coconut milk - 1/2 cup
- Onion - 1, sliced
- Lemongrass - 1 stalk, bruised
- Salt - to taste

Instructions:
1. Fry chicken until golden brown, then set aside.
2. In the same pan, cook onion and lemongrass until fragrant.
3. Add chili paste, tomato sauce, and coconut milk.
4. Add chicken back to the pan, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Season with salt and serve.
